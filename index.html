Symptoms of myocardial infarction

Chest pain
  • This is the most common first symptom and involves squeezing and uncomfortable pressure in the chest.
  • Pain accompanies this chest pain and is usually felt in the left arm but can also be felt in other areas of the body as well.
  • Shortness of breath
  • Sweating
  • Feeling weak and light-headed
  • Throwing up
  • Unconsciousness and even death due to the shortage of blood flow to the brain
    • Other Symptoms: Facts
  • 1/4 of cases can be silent without symptoms
  • Women are more likely to report numerous symptoms when having a heart attack
  • Symptoms can come gradually, over a span of a few minutes
  • Initial pain may be mistaken with the sensation of heartburn
